Can I use any kinds of headphones with my iPhone?

Short answer is yes. However, depending whether or not your iPhone has a headphone jack or not, you may need to buy an adapter to use the Lightning port (charging port) as a headphone jack. You can also use bluetooth headphones and go completely wireless as well.

Does the iPhone 8 Plus have Animoji?

Nope. Only iPhones that have the ‘true depth camera’ on the front have this capability. Any model released after the iPhone 8 Plus will have the feature to use Animoji, though.

How do I take a screenshot?

Press down the home screen and power button simultaneously to capture what’s on your image. You can acess the photo you took in the Photos app.

Can I use my Apple Pencil on my iPhone?

No: Apple pencils are only supported by iPads.

How can I adjust flashlight brightness on iPhone?

To adjust flashlight brightness, press on the flashlight icon in Control Center. The 3D Touch shortcut menu will show options for three levels of intensity. This will adjust the brightness of the rear camera flash LED to different levels.

Why won’t my iPhone screen rotate?

Chances are you've accidentally locked your iPhone into portrait orientation. As of iOS 7, Apple moved this feature into the Control Center, which slides up from the bottom of your iPhone screen.

What is the purpose of AirDrop?

Airdrop can transfer files of any size wirelessly, avoiding the need for email, messaging, external drives, or cables between devices to swap files. You can access it through the Photos app directly or hitting the ‘Share’ button anytime you want to use AirDrop.

Do I need a credit card to create an Apple ID?

No. You don’t need to input a credit card to create an Apple ID. However, if you intend to buy anything on the Apple Store or on iTunes, you will have to use either an Apple gift card or a credit card.
